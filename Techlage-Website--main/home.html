About Us...

Techlage Season 1, hosted by Legacy School's student tech team, is an event that aims to bring together the community and reiterate the importance of technology's place in our economy. Through this event, the tech team aspires to showcase the role of technology in solving real-world problems. Technology has been seamlessly integrated with education, career opportunities, hobbies, and for some, even in healthcare. It is this knowledge of the various real-world applications of technology that the student Tech team aims to share through the tech fest, Techlage.

Developed by Shayna Vinoth.
